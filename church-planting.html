Church Planting

"Frontline was birthed out of a passion for evangelism rooted in a commitment to the Great Commission."

We have a rich history of preaching the Gospel throughout the Philippine nation. For twenty years our mission was primarily taking the gospel message to the Filipino people through large gospel tent meetings, musical concerts, and stadium events. Throughout these years our mobile ministry team travelled to hundreds of towns and cities across the nation, reaching millions with the Good News of Jesus Christ.

Our core strategy for evangelism has since shifted to the vehicle of church planting, with an emphasis on rural areas where few or no active churches exist. Our growing multi-site church, Frontline Worship Center, has established numerous locations. Our vision is nationwide network of vibrant and engaging churches that effectively take the love and message of Christ to Filipinos everywhere, and that deeply impact the communities in which they exist.
